Overnight Oats

Overnight oats may be the most underrated breakfast food. Why? You can prepare a batch over the weekend and eat them the entire week. A meal-preppers dream!

One advantage to overnight oats is the variety. Below is just one of many recipes.

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Yogurt
  • Peanut Butter
  • Chia Seeds
  • Cinnamon
  1. Mix everything together in a mason jar.
  2. Put in fridge for at least 8 hours.
  3. Enjoy!

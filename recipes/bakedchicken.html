Baked Chicken

Chicken is one of the cheapest forms of protein which is ideal for frugal bodybuilders (like myself).

If someone like me can whip together some baked chicken in under an hour, than you can too!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices (as desired)

Instructions

  1. Lay out the breasts on a metal oven pan.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  3. Cover the breasts in oil and spices.
  4. Bake chicken breasts for 30 minutes.

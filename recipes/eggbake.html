Egg Bake

An egg bake is a simple, but awesome dish that requires minimal work and ingredients. It works well as a meal prep since all you need is a microwave to reheat

So what makes an egg bake so good? Just take a look below!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Turkey Sausage
  • Bread
  • Milk
  • Spinach
  • Olives
  • Spices (as desired)

Instructions

  1. Mix everything in a large bowl.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  3. Bake egg bake in oven for 35 minutes.
